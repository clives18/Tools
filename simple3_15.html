Welcome to Simple3 Plan

A practical business improvement framework that guides you through 41 targeted questions across five core areas — and generates a professional action plan you can start using today.

1

Complete the assessment

Answer up to 3 responses per question across Marketing, Growing, Operating, Managing, and Protecting. Helpful prompts support you throughout.

2

Set priorities and owners

Assign who owns each action using their initials. Priority and status are set in the Action Dashboard.

3

Review your dashboard

All actions flow automatically into your Action Dashboard, grouped by business area and filterable by status and priority.

4

Export your plan

Generate a clean, print-ready Business Improvement Plan to share with your team, advisors, or clients.
